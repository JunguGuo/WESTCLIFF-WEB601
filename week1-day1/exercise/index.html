De ludo scachorum

On the Game of Chess

"De ludo scachorum" translates to "On the Game of Chess" in English. It is one of the earliest works which discusses the game of chess in a systematic and analytical manner. The book contains various problems and solutions related to chess and was one of the early attempts to analyze the game mathematically.
